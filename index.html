About NCC

Our Mission

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is a youth development movement that has enormous potential for nation building.

The NCC provides opportunities to the youth of the country for their all-round development with a sense of commitment, dedication, self-discipline and moral values.

Training & Activities

Comprehensive training programs for holistic development

Drill Training

Master the fundamentals of military discipline and precision

Formation Marching
Command Response
Parade Training

Drill training forms the backbone of NCC activities, instilling discipline, coordination, and team spirit among cadets. Through regular practice, cadets develop precision in movement and quick response to commands.

Aeromodelling

Design, build, and fly model aircraft

Aircraft Design Principles
Model Construction
Flight Testing

Aeromodelling introduces cadets to the fascinating world of aviation through hands-on experience in building and flying model aircraft. This activity develops technical skills and understanding of aerodynamics.

Basic Flight Principles
Navigation Skills
Flight Safety

Flying training provides cadets with practical exposure to aviation. Through theoretical classes and practical sessions, cadets learn about aircraft operations, navigation, and flight safety procedures.

Weapon Handling
