Drill Training

Excellence in Precision and Discipline

Overview

Drill training is a fundamental aspect of NCC training that instills discipline, coordination, and teamwork among cadets. Through systematic practice and dedication, cadets develop the precision and discipline required for military-style formations and ceremonies.

Basic Drill Movements

  • Attention and Stand at Ease
  • Turning Movements
  • Marching and Halting
  • Saluting on the March
  • Squad Formation

Advanced Formations

  • Platoon Drill
  • Company Formation
  • Ceremonial Drill
  • Guard of Honor
  • Parade Ground Formation

Weapon Drill

  • Rifle Drill Movements
  • Arms Drill Commands
  • Weapon Handling
  • Ceremonial Arms Drill
  • Guard Mounting

Benefits of Drill Training

Mental Discipline

Develops focus, attention to detail, and quick response to commands

Team Coordination

Enhances synchronized movement and team awareness

Physical Fitness

Improves posture, stamina, and body coordination
