Drill Training

Master the art of precision and discipline through comprehensive drill training

Overview

Drill training is a fundamental aspect of NCC training that instills discipline, coordination, and teamwork among cadets. This comprehensive program covers various aspects of military drill movements and formations.

Training Components

Basic Drill Movements

  • Attention and Stand at Ease
  • Turning Movements
  • Marching in Different Speeds
  • Saluting and Courtesy

Advanced Formations

  • Line Formation
  • Column Formation
  • Square Formation
  • Circle Formation

Weapon Drill

  • Arms Drill
  • Weapon Handling
  • Safety Procedures
  • Maintenance

Benefits

Mental Discipline

Develops concentration and mental alertness

Team Coordination

Enhances teamwork and synchronization

Physical Fitness

Improves posture and physical endurance
