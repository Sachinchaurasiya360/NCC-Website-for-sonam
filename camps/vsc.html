About VSC

Learn about our premier aviation training program for Air Wing cadets.

The Vayu Sainik Camp (VSC) is a prestigious training program that offers Air Wing NCC cadets comprehensive exposure to various aspects of aviation and Air Force operations. This intensive camp combines theoretical knowledge with practical training, preparing cadets for potential careers in aviation.
