Selection Process

Our rigorous selection process identifies the most talented and dedicated cadets.

Initial Selection

  • Outstanding performance in institutional training
  • Minimum 'B' grade in 'B' Certificate
  • Physical fitness assessment

Group Level Selection

  • Inter-unit competitions
  • Drill evaluation
  • Cultural talent assessment

Directorate Level

  • Inter-group competitions
  • Leadership assessment
  • Final contingent selection

Pre-RDC Training

  • Intensive parade practice
  • Cultural performance rehearsals
  • Team building activities
