EBSB Camp

Camp Overview

Experience the rich cultural diversity through our unique exchange program.

Cultural Exchange

Learn and share regional art, dance, music, and traditions with cadets from paired states.

Language Learning

Basic language training in paired state's language to facilitate better communication.

Culinary Experience

Experience and learn about regional cuisines and cooking traditions.

Heritage Tours

Visit historical sites and learn about the rich heritage of paired states.

Selection Process

Our careful selection process ensures meaningful cultural exchange experiences.

Basic Eligibility

  • Second year NCC cadet
  • Good academic record
  • Active participation in unit activities

Cultural Assessment

  • Knowledge of state culture
  • Performing arts abilities
  • Communication skills

Group Selection

  • Team coordination assessment
  • Leadership qualities
  • Adaptability evaluation

Final Selection

  • Interview with commanding officer
  • Medical fitness check
  • Parent/Guardian consent
