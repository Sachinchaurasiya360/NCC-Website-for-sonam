Selection Process

Our comprehensive selection process ensures that cadets are well-prepared for the challenges of desert survival and adventure.

Basic Requirements

  • Second/Third year NCC cadet
  • Physical fitness certification
  • Medical fitness clearance

Physical Assessment

  • Endurance test
  • Heat tolerance assessment
  • Basic swimming ability

Technical Evaluation

  • Map reading skills
  • Basic first aid knowledge
  • Communication abilities

Final Clearance

  • Commanding officer approval
  • Parent/Guardian consent
  • Equipment check
